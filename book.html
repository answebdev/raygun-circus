Terminal Fiction: Selected Lyrics and Words (1995-2017)

A collection of lyrics and words written by Adolf N.S. between 1995 and 2017. Writer, vocalist, and guitarist for Raygun Circus, he has also written and recorded solo music as A.N.S. and Cori Celesti, and has also played with Tactical Fever and Street of Little Girls. Other past bands and collaborations include T.O.E. and The Marauding Band of Cut-Throats. Terminal Fiction is a selection of lyrics and words written for these bands and collaborations, and includes a selection of lyrics and words that were never used, or that were written for songs that were never recorded.
